HOW IT WORKS
FINDMYSCHOOL WORKS IN FOUR SIMPLE STEPS
1 - SEARCH
On the Homepage, locate the Search box and input the name or the location of the school you are looking for, and click on the search button. Alternatively, click on the school link on the top menu to view the list of all schools.
2 - LOCATE
Get to see where the school is located by clicking on the school profile. It's even more exciting, you can get the direction from anywhere in the country.
3 - APPLY
Apply for your child's interview to the school of your choice. Click on the APPLY button to get the appliction form. We also follow up on the application to make sure you get a reply on time.
4 - RELAX
Once your child gets adimitted. you can now relax. You can check out our blog to see what we recommend when school hunting.
